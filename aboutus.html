More Than a Restaurant

SOMAA Restobar was built on a single belief: that a great evening out should engage every sense. We set out to create a space in Visakhapatnam where extraordinary food, handcrafted drinks, and live music could come together effortlessly — night after night.

From our flagship venue on VIP Road, Siripuram, to our Yendada branch, every SOMAA location is designed to feel welcoming yet premium — familiar yet exciting. We take pride in every detail, from the first greeting to the final bite.

  • Sourced ingredients, cooked with care and creativity
  • A craft bar programme built around bold, balanced flavours
  • Live performances that make every visit feel like an occasion
  • A team trained to make guests feel genuinely at home
